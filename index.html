About Jacki

I love designing solutions. After a quick 10-year stint coaching college sports, I’m pivoting my career to focus on helping clients build a better brand with a purposeful web presence that connects them to their users. I bring a wide range of experience into every job, including years of experience on the other side of the screen - wishing I had better products to use!

I am a great team player, a fierce competitor, a problem solver by nature and constantly looking for opportunities to increase efficiency. I love to build – teams, visions, strategies, products – and I believe that even something good can always been made better.

What I love about design:
